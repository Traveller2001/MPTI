# JIRA 项目永动机 — 文生图 Prompt

## 主 Prompt

A cute 3D chibi-style cartoon character on a pure white background, clay render aesthetic. A smiling but relentless middle-aged professor wearing glasses and a business casual outfit, standing on top of a mountain of project folders and documents. He holds a huge Kanban board / project task board filled with colorful sticky notes in one hand, and points forward with the other as if assigning more tasks. Around him float speech bubbles saying "再接一个横向", "这个项目你来做", "毕业论文不急". Tiny exhausted students around him are each carrying stacks of project folders, laptops, and documents labeled "横向项目", "企业合作", "项目验收". One small student in the corner is looking sadly at a dusty graduation thesis labeled "毕业论文" covered in cobwebs. The professor's desk has a project management screen showing an endless backlog. Soft studio lighting, high detail, isometric perspective, Pixar-style 3D rendering.

## 风格要求

- **风格**: 3D Q版/粘土渲染（参考现有图的 Pixar-like chibi 风格）
- **背景**: 纯白色，无场景
- **构图**: 主角色居中放大，配角/道具围绕分布
- **文字气泡**: 中文，手写/卡通字体，2-4个
- **输出格式**: PNG + WebP，透明或白底均可
- **尺寸**: 与现有图一致（建议 1024x1024 或更高）

## 风格参考

- 参考 `../PUSH/PUSH.png` 的布局风格（角色举着道具 + 脚下堆满东西）
- 核心道具：看板/任务板、横向项目文件夹堆、蒙灰的毕业论文
- 表情：精力充沛、指点江山（和学生的疲惫形成反差）
- 重点细节：角落里一个小学生望着落灰的毕业论文，营造"项目做不完，论文没法写"的氛围
