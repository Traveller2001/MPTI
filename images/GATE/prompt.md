# GATE 实习守门员 — 文生图 Prompt

## 主 Prompt

A cute 3D chibi-style cartoon character on a pure white background, clay render aesthetic. A stern middle-aged male professor wearing glasses and a dark suit, standing in front of a giant locked iron gate. He holds a large red stamp that says "不批准" (Not Approved) in one hand, and an internship application form crumpled in the other. Around him float speech bubbles saying "实习？先把课题做完", "你的论文呢？", "实习不算学分". At his feet are piles of rejected internship applications with red X marks, a broken chain, and a padlock. Behind the gate, tiny students in graduation caps are reaching through the bars trying to grab briefcases labeled "实习offer". The character has a stern, blocking posture with arms crossed or hand raised in a "stop" gesture. Soft studio lighting, high detail, isometric perspective, Pixar-style 3D rendering.

## 风格要求

- **风格**: 3D Q版/粘土渲染（参考现有图的 Pixar-like chibi 风格）
- **背景**: 纯白色，无场景
- **构图**: 主角色居中放大，配角/道具围绕分布
- **文字气泡**: 中文，手写/卡通字体，2-4个
- **输出格式**: PNG + WebP，透明或白底均可
- **尺寸**: 与现有图一致（建议 1024x1024 或更高）

- 参考 `../996/996.png` 的布局风格（大角色居中 + 周围小学生 + 气泡文字）

- 小学生们隔着铁栅栏伸手够实习offer，形成对比
